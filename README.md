# George FIT — UX Redesign Case Study

Two UX case studies redesigning features within **George**, the digital banking app by Erste Bank — one of Austria's largest financial institutions.

**Design by Simay Uslu · [simayy.com](https://simayy.com)**

---

## Case Studies

### 1. George FIT Redesign
`george-fit-case-study.html` — Redesign of the fitness and wellbeing feature inside the George banking app, connecting financial health with physical health goals.

### 2. Spending Insights
`george-spending-insights-case-study.html` — Redesign of the spending analytics dashboard, improving how users understand and act on their financial data.
